## Overview

**Random Youtube App** helps you watch YouTube with less searching and fewer distractions. Choose a category, play a random video, and move through videos with simple controls.

It is useful for English practice, music discovery, kids and baby songs, background listening, and saving useful videos for later.

## Key Features

- **Random playback** from selected video categories
- **Grouped categories** for easier browsing
- **English learning content** for listening, speaking, TOEIC, IELTS, and CEFR levels
- **Music categories** for songs, remixes, karaoke, running music, and relaxing listening
- **Kids and baby content** such as infant songs, nursery music, and gentle listening
- **Watch history** to replay videos you recently watched
- **Favorite library** to save videos you like
- **Previous and next controls** for quick navigation
- **Light and dark mode** for comfortable viewing
- **Sleep timer** for music or listening before rest
- **Loop controls** to repeat useful video sections
- **QR sharing** to open the current video on another device
- **Dictionary and translation tools** for learning support

## Useful For

### English Learning

Use the app for daily listening practice, speaking exposure, podcasts, TOEIC, IELTS, and level-based English videos.

### Music Listening

Play random songs, remixes, karaoke tracks, running music, or background music without manually searching each time.

### Kids And Baby Music

Quickly start gentle songs, infant music, nursery rhymes, and baby-friendly listening categories.

### Focused Practice

Use loop controls to repeat a section of a video, save helpful videos to favorites, and return to watched videos through history.

## Why This App Exists

YouTube is powerful, but searching and recommendations can become distracting. Random Youtube App keeps the experience simple:

1. Pick a category.
2. Play a random video.
3. Save, skip, repeat, or continue watching.

The goal is to make video discovery faster and more useful for everyday learning, listening, and relaxing.

## Privacy Notes

The app does not require an account. Favorites, watch history, and recent selections are stored in your browser.

YouTube videos are played through YouTube, so YouTube's own policies and tracking may still apply when videos are loaded.
